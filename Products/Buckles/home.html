Custom Belt Buckles

  • Quality cast custom coins - any design - 3D modeling available

  • Corporate logo coins for promotion and recognition, as well as military challenge coins

  • Zinc and pewter coins available in sizes from 1" to 8"

  • Die-Struck available from 1" to 3"

  • Die-Cast available from 1" to 5"

  • Most common sizes are 1-1/4" to 1-1/2"

If you’re looking for a quality, value, product for your club membership, corporate presentation, promotion, ceremony, fundraiser, wedding, or just for a fun giveaway – our custom Belt Buckles are an incredible choice.


We’ve been making personalized Belt Buckles here in our Canadian factory for over 47 years! Our custom Belt Buckles have been shipped around the world for Military, Government, big Brands, even high profile events we can’t talk about!


We recognize that custom Buckles aren’t for everyone. Our Belt Buckles are different. They are not stock items shipped from China (although we do have forged relationships with overseas factories). We understand that not everyone wants a buckle in standard or traditional shapes and sizes. That’s why we manufacture your custom Belt Buckles in any shape, size, design, finish, and with any feature you want.


All our Belt Buckles are completely manufactured for you at our state of the art factory. Our team of true artists and craftsmen look at every custom Buckle design with fresh eyes. So your custom Belt Buckle order is unique and personal. We work with your logo, your photo – (yes, we can turn photographs into three dimensional Belt Buckles!)


We specialize in large and small orders. Our reputation for customer satisfaction is something we work seriously hard to maintain. Our process is simple and straight forward. Every custom Buckle customer receives an art preview and an accurate quote - no surprises! We work with you, we impress you. What we say is what we do!
