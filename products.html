Our Products

At our company, we specialize in manufacturing custom domestic spin cast products that can be made with or without color added. We also offer overseas die-cast and die-struck custom products for our clients. We take pride in offering wholesale pricing to our clients, which helps them to be more competitive in the market.

We understand that when it comes to ordering custom products, ease of ordering, reliable delivery, fast service, and affordability are important factors. That is why we prioritize these aspects when serving our clients. Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality custom products that meet our clients' specific needs.

Over the past 47 years, we have completed tens of thousands of orders for various industries. Some of our notable works include sports awards, military challenge coins and buckles, corporate branding, advertising specialties, promotional products, corporate recognition and awards. Our experience ranges from large marathon events to small club projects, which means we have seen almost everything!

We offer a minimum order size starting at 25 pieces on many of our products. This makes it easier for small businesses and organizations to order custom products that fit their needs without worrying about meeting large minimum order requirements.

If you have a project that requires custom products, please do not hesitate to contact us. Our team is always ready to listen and provide solutions that meet your needs. We are committed to ensuring that our clients get the best value for their money while delivering high-quality products that meet their expectations.
